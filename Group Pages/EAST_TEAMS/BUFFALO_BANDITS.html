Meet The Team



The Buffalo Bandits are a professional lacrosse team based in Buffalo, New York. They are one of the oldest and most successful teams in the National Lacrosse League (NLL), having been founded in 1992 and winning four Champion's Cup championships in their history. They have a dedicated fan base and play their home games at the KeyBank Center, which is one of the largest and most iconic indoor arenas in the league.

The Bandits have a talented roster of players, including some of the biggest names in the sport. Known for their exciting, fast-paced style of play, with games often featuring high-scoring affairs and thrilling finishes, the Buffalo Bandits are a storied and exciting team that any lacrosse fan should be sure to check out.
