The Bandits are Stealing the Show

The Buffalo Bandits are a lacrosse powerhouse that's sure to get your blood pumping! Based in Buffalo, New York, this storied team has been a force to be reckoned with in the National Lacrosse League (NLL) since its inception in 1992. With a winning tradition that includes four league championships and numerous division titles, the Bandits have cemented their place as one of the most exciting and dominant teams in the league. Led by a talented roster of players and an experienced coaching staff, the Bandits are known for their high-octane offense, bone-crunching defense, and never-say-die attitude. So if you're ready for some heart-stopping lacrosse action, get ready to cheer on the Buffalo Bandits!
