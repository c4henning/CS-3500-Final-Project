Meet The Team



The Georgia Swarm are a professional lacrosse team that competes in the National Lacrosse League (NLL). In 2017, they became the NLL World Champions and brought Atlanta its first professional world championship in 22 years.

The team plays its home games at Harrah's Cherokee Casino Field at Infinite Energy Arena in Duluth, Georgia, and the season runs from December to April, with playoffs in May and June. The Swarm has been operating in Atlanta for five seasons and is owned by John Arlotta and Andy Arlotta.
