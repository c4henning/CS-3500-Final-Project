Meet The Team



The Albany Firewolves are a professional lacrosse team based in Albany, New York. The team was originally the New England Black Wolves. The Black Wolves was originally a relocation of The Philadelphia Wings but the Philadelphia Wings have since reentered the National Lacrosse League.

The Albany Firewolves have officailly started playing in the National Lacrosse League in the 2021-2022 season. They started their first season off strong with a respectable 9-9 record. This record led them to the Quarterfinals of the National Lacrosse League where they lost to the Buffalo Bandits 5 - 10.

Although the team has started off struggling this season, you must remember that they are a new team. Coming into this season they got two high overall draft picks that they are trying to incorporate into their team. This young team has potential to go to the moon.
