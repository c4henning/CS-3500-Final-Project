Meet The Team

The Toronto Rock are based in Hamilton, Ontario, Canada and are members of the Eastern Conference of the National Lacrosse League (NLL). They were the first Canadian frnachise in the NLL. The current owner and general manager of the team is Jamie Dawick. The team was founded in 1998 as the Ontario Raiders in Hamilton and played at Copps Coliseum before they were sold to a group of investors who relocated the franchise to Toronto. They were then renamed the "Toronto Rock", and started playing at Maple Leaf Gardens in 1999. In 2001, the Rock moved to Scotiabank Arena where they played for 20 seasons. In May of 2021, the team announced the relocation from Scotiabank Arena in Toronto to FirstOntario Centre in Hamilton. They continue to represent Toronto while playing home games in Hamilton.
