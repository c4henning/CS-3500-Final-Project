Meet The Team

The Philadelphia Wings are a professional box lacrosse team and part of the National Lacrosse League East Division. They play at the Wells Fargo Center in Philadelphia. The Wings were one of the original six members of the first National Lacrosse League and one of the original teams in the Eagle Pro Box Lacrosse League. The team moved to Connecticut in 2014 but a new team started in 2018, bringing the Wings back to Philly.
