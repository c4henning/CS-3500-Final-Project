THE ROCHESTER KNIGHTHAWKS

Meet The Team



The Knighthawks were originally founded in 1995 and played in the NLL until 2019 when they were sold and moved to Halifax, Nova Scotia, becoming the Halifax Thunderbirds. However, the Knighthawks were re-established in 2020 as an expansion team and have since been playing in the league once again.

Since their reformation, the Knighthawks have been working to build a new team from scratch. They have brought in experienced players, coaches, and front office personnel to help with the rebuilding process. In their first season back, the Knighthawks struggled and finished with a record of 2 wins and 10 losses.

However, the team has been making strides to improve and build towards the future. They have added young talent through the NLL draft and have continued to develop their roster. The Knighthawks are hoping to make a strong return to the NLL playoffs in the near future and compete for a championship once again.
