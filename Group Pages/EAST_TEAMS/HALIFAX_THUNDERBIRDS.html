THE HALIFAX THUNDERBIRDS

Meet The Team



The Halifax Thunderbirds, based in Nova Scotia, were founded in 2018 and joined the National Lacrosse League (NLL) as an expansion team for the 2019-2020 season. The Thunderbirds play their home games at the Scotiabank Centre in downtown Halifax, which has a seating capacity of over 10,000.

Since joining the league, the Thunderbirds have quickly made a name for themselves as one of the top teams in the NLL. In their first season, they finished with a league-best record of 12-4 and made it to the NLL Finals, where they were ultimately defeated by the Calgary Roughnecks. Despite the loss, the Thunderbirds' inaugural season was widely regarded as a huge success, both on and off the field.

The Thunderbirds' success has been driven by a talented roster of players and a dedicated coaching staff. Led by head coach Mike Accursi, the team has built a culture of hard work, discipline, and a commitment to excellence. The Thunderbirds' players are known for their skill, toughness, and passion for the game, and they have quickly become fan favorites in Halifax and around the league. With a bright future ahead of them, the Halifax Thunderbirds are poised to continue their success for years to come.
