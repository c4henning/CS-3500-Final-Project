Meet The Team

The Calgary Roughnecks are a professional box lacrosse team in the National Lacrosse League West and were founded in 2001. Located in WestJet Field in the Scotiabank Saddledome in Calgary, the Roughnecks got their name from the hardworking oil drillers found throughout Alberta. They've claimed the NLL Cup three times.
