The Las Vegas Desert Dogs

The Las Vegas Desert Dogs is a National Lacrosse League team based in the US city of Las Vegas. They are owned by a couple big names in other sports such as Steve Nash, the eight-time NBA All-Star, and Wayne Gretzky, the undisputed greatest hockey player of all time. The Desert Dogs represent being "scrappy and cunning, friendly but feirce, and poweful alone but unstoppable as a pack."
