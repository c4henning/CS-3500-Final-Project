Meet The Team

The Colorado Mammoths are a professional box lacrosse team in the National Lacrosse League West Division. They were originally founded as the Baltimore Thunder in 1987. In 1999 they moved to Pitsburgh, then D.C before settling at Ball Stadium in Denver, Colorado, in 2003 where they have been ever since. The Mammoths have won two NLL Cups including during the 2022 NLL championship and are currently the defending champs.
