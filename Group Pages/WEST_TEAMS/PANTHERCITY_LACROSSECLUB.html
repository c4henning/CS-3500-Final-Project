Meet The Team



The Panther City Lacrosse Club is a new addition to the National Lacrosse League, joining the league in 2021. The team is based in Fort Worth, Texas, and is owned by Bill Cameron and Greg Bibb.

The team's first game was on December 4, 2021, against the Saskatchewan Rush, which they won 14-12 in an exciting matchup. The Panther City Lacrosse Club has quickly gained a loyal fanbase in Fort Worth and is poised to become a major contender in the NLL in the years to come.
