Meet The Team

The San Diego Seals are based in San Diego, California and are a part of the Western Conference of the National Lacrosse League (NLL). They were founded in August of 2017 and the 2018-2019 season was their inaugural season. They play their home games in Pechanga Arena in San Diego.
