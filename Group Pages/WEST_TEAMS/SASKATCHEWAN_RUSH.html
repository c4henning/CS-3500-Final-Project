The Saskatchewan Rush

The Saskatchewan Rush are based in Saskatoon, Saskatchewan, Canada and are a part of the Western Conference of the National Lacrosse League (NLL). Formerly known as the Edmonton Rush, the team became the Saskatchewan Rush when they relocated from Edmonton to Saskatoon in 2016. They play their home games on Co-op Field at SaskTel Centre.
