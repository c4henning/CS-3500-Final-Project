The Vancouver Warriors

The Vancouver Warriors are based in Vancouver, British Columbia and are a part of the Western Conference of the National Lacrosse League (NLL). The Warriors were originally known as the Albany Attack until 2003 when they moved to San Jose and adopted the name Stealth. They kept this name until they were sold to Canucks Sports & Entertainment who rebranded the team to the Warriors in 2018. They play their home games at Rogers Arena in Vancouver, British Columbia.
